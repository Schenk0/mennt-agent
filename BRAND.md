# Mennt

---

## 1. Vision

Mennt exists to make owning and updating a simple business website effortless.

We believe small businesses should not need to:
- Hire a developer for every small change  
- Learn complex no-code tools  
- Re-learn how a platform works months after building their site  

Mennt makes website updates as simple as describing what you want in plain language.

---

## 2. Mission

To become the simplest way for small businesses to create and maintain a professional online presence — powered by AI, controlled by humans.

---

## 3. The Problem

Small businesses face a recurring website problem:

1. They need a simple online presence (landing page, services, contact info).
2. They either:
  - Hire a developer (expensive + dependent), or  
  - Use tools like WordPress or Wix (complex over time).
3. Months later, they need to:
  - Change opening hours  
  - Add a service  
  - Update pricing  
  - Replace images  
4. They no longer remember how to use the tool — or they return to paying someone.

This creates:
- Frustration  
- Ongoing costs  
- Dependency  
- Stagnant websites  

Mennt removes this friction entirely.

---

## 4. The Solution

Mennt is an AI-powered website builder focused exclusively on simple company and landing pages.

Core concept:
Users describe changes in natural language.  
AI modifies the underlying code automatically.

Example:
- “Change our opening hours to 9–6 on weekdays.”
- “Add a testimonials section under services.”
- “Replace the hero image with something more modern.”
- “Make the page feel more premium.”

No drag-and-drop.  
No complex dashboards.  
No relearning tools.  

Just:
Describe → Review → Publish.

---

## 5. What Mennt Is Not

Mennt is intentionally limited.

It is NOT:
- A full-stack product builder
- A database-driven web app platform
- A complex SaaS builder
- A replacement for developer-heavy tools
- A competitor to advanced AI dev platforms like Bolt or Lovable

Mennt focuses on one thing:
Simple, elegant, static business websites.

Depth is replaced by clarity.

---

## 6. Target Audience

Primary:
- Local businesses
- Freelancers
- Consultants
- Agencies with simple service offerings
- Non-technical founders

Common traits:
- Need credibility online
- Don’t want technical complexity
- Make occasional updates
- Value time over customization depth

---

## 7. Positioning

Mennt is:

The AI website builder for small businesses that don’t want to learn website builders.

Category:
AI-powered simple website maintenance platform.

Market Position:
Simpler than traditional no-code tools.  
More focused than AI dev platforms.  
More empowering than hiring a freelancer for every change.

---

## 8. Core Principles

### 1. Radical Simplicity
If a feature increases cognitive load, it must justify its existence.

### 2. Natural Language First
The primary interface is conversation, not configuration panels.

### 3. Human-Controlled AI
AI suggests and edits.  
Users review and approve.

### 4. Opinionated Design
Clean templates.  
Professional layouts.  
No overwhelming customization.

### 5. Maintenance > Creation
Building the site is step one.  
Updating it effortlessly for years is the real product.

---

## 9. Product Philosophy

Mennt should feel like:

- Talking to a competent web designer  
- Not operating a tool  
- Not configuring software  

The interface should feel:
- Calm  
- Clear  
- Focused  
- Non-technical  

Every feature must answer:
“Does this help a small business owner update their site faster and with less stress?”

If not, it does not belong.

---

## 10. Long-Term Vision

Mennt becomes:

- The default online presence tool for small businesses  
- The AI layer between non-technical owners and website code  
- A platform where developers can design the initial site, and owners maintain it effortlessly  

In the long term:
- Developers can offer “Mennt-compatible” site designs  
- Agencies can use Mennt for clients who don’t need complex systems  
- Businesses feel independent without becoming technical
